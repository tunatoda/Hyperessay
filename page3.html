ALBERT EINSTEIN PASSES AT 76



Born 1879 - died 1955.

Death by internal bleeding— ruptured abdominal aortic aneurysm.

One of the most influencial scientists of all time, Einstein recieved a Nobel Prize in physics amongst many other accolades and awards for the many world-changing discoveries in his time alive.

On his death bed, he uttered his last words in German to a nearby nurse. She did not speak the language.

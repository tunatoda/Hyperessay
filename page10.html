THE SELF-IMMOLATION OF AARON BUSHNELL



Born 1998 - died 2024
Death by self immolation.

Aaron Bushnell was a 25 year old military serviceman who set himself on fire in front of the Embassy of Isreal in Washington, DC as a form of protest against the conflict taking place in Palestine. He repeated his last words as he burned until his death.

The livestreamed event caused uproar worldwide, with many drawing comparisons to the self-immolation of Thích Quảng Đức in 1963, a similar act of extreme protest and call for action that took place in Vietnam.

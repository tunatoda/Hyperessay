BARBARA WALTERS DEAD AT 93



Born 1929 - died 2022

Death by natural causes.

Barbara Jill Walters was an American TV personality featured on many channels from the 20th to the 21st century, including NBC, the ABC Evening News, 20/20, and The View. Despite retiring in May 2013, Walters continued supporting multiple channels as a writer and occasioanlly as a special guest.

She passed peacefully at her home in Manhattan. Despite having suffered from dimentia in the late stages of her life, she maintained that she lived a great life— her words now etched onto her gravestone.

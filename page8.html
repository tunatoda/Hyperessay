THE DEATH OF A NOMAD



Born 1968 - died 1992

Presumed death by starvation.

McCandless was an American adventurer who lived a primarily nomadic lifestyle in North America. He mainly stayed in the Alaskan wilderness, residing in an abandoned bus which he made into his shelter. Before his death, he photographed himself holding a note with his last words and waving towards the camera.

His story went on to be retold many times, including in the 2007 documentary "Into the Wild."

"Dear world, I am leaving because
I am bored. I feel I have lived long
enough. I am leaving you with your
worries in this sweet cesspool —
good luck.”
-George Sanders,
Apr 25, 1972


TRAGIC DEATH OF GEORGE SANDERS



Born 1906 - died 1972.

Death by suicide.

During Sanders' final years, he faced 3 deaths in his family, including that of his third wife, all in the span of 1 year. Many other hardships brought on by his developing dementia, more deaths, and other losses in his life sent him into a deep depression.

His last words listed above were written on one of two suicide notes he left behind.

LAST WORDS SPOKEN ON THE MOON



Spoken on Apollo 17 Mission.

Cernan was the 11th and final man to walk on the lunar surface. On his third and final expedition, he made the speech.

He was born on Mar 14, 1934, and died on Jan 16, 2017 at a hospital in Houston. Cernan passed to natural causes and was buried with full military honors.

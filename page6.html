GILMORE FACES DEATH TODAY


Born 1940 - died 1977.

Death by state execution— firing squad.

Gary Gilmore was an American criminal who earned his death sentence following his convicted crimes of two murders.
His case gained national attention after becoming the first person in America to demand the death penalty for himself.

Later in 1988, the founder of the Wieden Kennedy advertising agency Dan Wielden would use Gilmore’s last words as
inspiration for the now iconic trademark tagline of Nike— rephrased now as “Just do it.”

THE FIRST & LAST WORDS TO THE UNIVERSE


Voyager probe, launched in 1977.

The Golden Record aboard the Voyager probe is a time capsule dedicated for any possible extraterrestrial life that may stumble upon it. The speech above by Jimmy Carter, the 39th president of the United states (1977-1981), are amongst the first and as of now last words to the universe.

Alongside Carter's speech, other pieces of audio are also on the disc, such as greetings spoken by children around the world in 55 languages, as well as engravings detailing human reproduction, coordinates to our planet, and a collection of 116 images and natural sounds.
